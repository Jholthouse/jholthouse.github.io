The Queer Word, Netart, 2018

jholthouse.github.io/netart

For this piece I wanted to explore the idea of sharing knowledge. More specifically information regarding queer culture. In this piece I have provided a mixture of one hundred and forty four examples of words plus definitions, coming out stories, pictures of hate crimes, and significant people who have moved the queer community forward. In this piece I have randomly hidden this information behind a beautiful array of colored squares to represent the hidden aspects to this fringe community. I believe that information, communication, and sharing of feelings are the most important part of acceptance and this piece is the embodiment of that idea.
